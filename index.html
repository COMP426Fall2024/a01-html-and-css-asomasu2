In 2022, the Golden State Warriors went on an impressive run to win the NBA championship. The Warriors were no strangers to NBA titles, but it had been a couple years since they had won, making everyone question whether or not they had it in them to contend for another. What makes this championship so special not only included in season events and accolades, but also the previous years downturns. For example, the Warriors missed the playoffs in

  1. 2019-2020
  2. 2020-2021
due to a number of reasons, one of which being Klay Thompsons absence from the team for both of those seasons. Stephen Curry also only played 5 games in the 2019-2020 season, adding insult to injury.
